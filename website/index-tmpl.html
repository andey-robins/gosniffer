CEDAR WiFi Sniffer

What is this?
This page shows all the different wifi networks that are being looked for by all the devices around us. When a wifi device is not connected to its favorite network it sends out a broadcast looking for all the different wifi networks it has connected to in the past. This information can be used in conjunction with a war-driving database which is a database of wifi signal names and their geographic location. This can be dangerous information if used by the wrong people as it can provide a map of your favorite hangout spots.

How do I fix this?
- Check your wifi settings to see if you have a randomized network name setting
- Delete any remembered networks that you rarely connect to
- Turn off wifi when you know you're not trying to connect to one
